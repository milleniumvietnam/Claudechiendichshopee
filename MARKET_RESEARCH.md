## Trending Products on Shopee Vietnam

### Tier 1: Ultra-Hot (Sales spike, high margins)

**1. Wireless Earbuds/Headphones**
- Avg price: $20-50 (₫450k-1.2M)
- Sales volume: 500-5,000+ units/month per seller
- Margin: 25-35% (wholesale ₫300k → retail ₫800k)
- Customer rating: 4.5-5.0 stars

- **Why**: Gen Z addiction to music + on-the-go listening

**2. Power Banks (10k-30k mAh)**
- Avg price: $8-25 (₫180k-600k)
- Sales volume: 1,000-10,000+ units/month per seller
- Margin: 30-40%
- Customer rating: 4.6-4.9 stars
- Competition: **MEDIUM** (20-30 active sellers)
- **Why**: Phone battery anxiety, travel essential

**3. Smartwatch Accessories (Bands, Chargers)**
- Avg price: $5-15 (₫110k-350k)
- Sales volume: 300-2,000 units/month per seller
- Margin: 40-50% (highest margin!)
- Customer rating: 4.7-5.0 stars
- Competition: **LOW** (5-10 active sellers)
- **Why**: Growing smartwatch adoption, low-cost add-on

**4. Fast-Charge Phone Chargers (65W-120W)**

**5. Phone Cases (Premium/Designer)**

## Pricing Architecture

### Wholesale Pricing (Buy From Supplier)

**Wireless Earbuds**
- Wholesale: ₫300k-400k per unit
- Retail (1x markup): ₫800k-1M
- Potential margin: 25-35%

**Power Banks**
- Wholesale: ₫120k-180k
- Retail (1.5x-2x): ₫250k-350k
- Potential margin: 30-45%

**Smartwatch Bands**
- Wholesale: ₫50k-80k
- Retail (2-3x): ₫150k-250k
- Potential margin: 40-60%

**Smart Home Items**
- Wholesale: ₫200k-500k
- Retail (1.5-2x): ₫400k-1M
- Potential margin: 35-50%

### Recommended Strategy: Premium Tier

Instead of competing on low prices, position as **"Curated Premium"**:

```
Wholesale Cost: ₫100k
Add: Packaging/Content: ₫20k
Add: Margin/Profit: ₫80k
RETAIL PRICE: ₫200k (vs ₫150k generic)

Justification: "Verified quality, guaranteed authentic, 30-day return"
```

**Result:** Higher margins (40-50%), less competition, better customer loyalty.
